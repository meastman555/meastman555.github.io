About Me

Student. Gamer. President. Pianist.

Hello! I’m Mason, a junior at the University of Texas at Austin studying Computer Science. I’ve a passion for programming, math, and all things video-games. I’ve played everything from Wii Sports to Red Dead Redemption 2; my dream is to work on rich, narrative-driven RPGs. Bethesda’s Creation Kit and the Unity game engine have given me a taste for what the world of game development offers — and I want more. I love to learn about game development, through GDC industry talks, and events — ranging from industry speaker nights to hands-on workshops — with the game development club on campus, of which I am currently the president. As a computer science major, my focus is in programming, though I have done a fair share of game design and even management while working on group projects. However, I am eager to learn and practice all aspects of game development. I excel at working with other people, and there isn’t a problem I won’t chase down until completion. When I’m not coding, I’m always thinking of what would be fun not only to play, but to create.

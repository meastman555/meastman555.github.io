Space Game!!!

In space, no one can hear you (or your friend) scream.

Space Game!!! is a game created for the Global Game Jam in early 2020 -- "repair" was the theme. The goal of the game is to work cooperatively with another player (locally) to pilot an astronaut and ship around levels to find and repair a broken satellie, through quicktime input. If the ship hits a wall or an asteroid, the astronaut must fly in and repair the ship in the same way. My team at this jam was comprised of most of my teammates on Endangered, along with a few new faces. My role for this game was programmer. During the weekend, I was responsible for writing C# scripts that handled controller input, movement, transitions, and the main menu. In addition I helped with some in-engine programming. Slightly ambitious, this project pushed the boundaries of what a group of beginners could accomplish in one weekend.

Awards: Best Multiplayer/Cooperative game (out of 30+ jam submissions).

Controls: Astronaut: (Xbox Controller) Left stick to move, X to increase tether, B to decrease tether, face buttons for quick-time input; (Keyboard) I-J-K-L for movement. This happened very late due to build errors, so the other controls were not mapped in time. Spaceship: (Xbox Controller) Triggers to turn, A to move forward; (Keyboard) A-D to turn, Spacebar to move forward.

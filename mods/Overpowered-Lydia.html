Overpowered Lydia

For when the best follower can't quite protect themselves enough.

Followers in Skyrim die too quickly, and often accidentally by the player’s own hand. This always frustrated me, but instead of downloading an already made mod to fix this, I decided to learn how to do it myself, while making a few additional changes in the process. I chose to buff Lydia, because you meet her early in the game, and she is a well balanced, valuable follower (and a lovable meme, but that’s not as important). I dramatically scaled her melee weapon up, while making her invulnerable. Does this break the game a little bit? Yes. Does it make obliterating dragons THAT much better? Absolutely.

A simple mod, all I had to do was manipulate Lydia’s NPC through the Creation Kit; no scripting or external assets were needed.

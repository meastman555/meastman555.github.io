Paul Rudd Follower

He’s Ant Man, an acclaimed actor, and now … a Skyrim follower?

This mod allows the Dragonborn to be accompanied by a fully-voiced Elf Paul, complete with full Elvish armor and enchanted weapons. He can be found in the Bannered Mare, and will accompany you in fights at range using his bow or up close with his dagger.

A decent amount of work went into creating this, as it was the first mod I was attempting more than simple manipulation of existing weapons/assets. I had to first design a custom NPC in-engine and allow for the game to treat it as a follower. Then I had to make sure the player could interact with my NPC in all the ways they would interact with a vanilla follower. Next came Paul’s custom armor, weapons, and behavior in combat. The engine provided the settings and traits I would need to manipulate, but I still had to learn which values and boxes corresponded to which behaviors and traits in-game. My challenge was uncovering the in-game dialogue that needed to be edited, rewriting it, and replacing the audio with trimmed clips of Paul Rudd from the internet. I also had to ensure my updates were correctly linked to only my new NPC.
